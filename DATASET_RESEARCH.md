# RLHF Dataset Research Report
## Deep Analysis of 21+ Sources for 3-Stage Alignment Pipeline

**Date:** June 2025  
**Hardware:** 4x Tesla K80 (11 GB each, fp32 only)  
**Model:** TinyLlama-1.1B (Llama architecture, compatible with transformers 4.38.2)

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Each dataset scored on:
- **Relevance** (to our SFT→RM→PPO pipeline)
- **Quality** (human vs synthetic, annotation methodology)
- **Size** (fit for K80 memory/time constraints)
- **Format** (ease of use with HuggingFace datasets)
- **License** (permissive vs restricted)

## K80-Specific Considerations

1. **Memory Budget**: TinyLlama-1.1B at fp32 ≈ 4.4 GB. With LoRA adapters + optimizer states, fits in single K80's 11 GB
2. **Tokenization**: Max sequence length 512 tokens to stay within VRAM
3. **Batch Size**: Likely batch_size=1 with gradient_accumulation=16 on each GPU
4. **Training Time**: With 4x K80 DataParallel, SFT on 20K samples ≈ 12-24 hours
5. **Subsetting**: For initial experiments, use 10-20K samples from ultrachat_200k
